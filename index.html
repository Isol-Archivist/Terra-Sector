I. TERRA SECTOR — LAND DOMINION

The Hosts of the
Imperial Federation

"Each Host is not merely a military unit, but a cultural banner, a marching nation unto itself, embodying its own martial philosophy beneath the Shahanshah's golden standard."

The Land Armies of Isol are organized into seven mighty Hosts, vast enough to wage independent campaigns across the breadth of the Golden Continent. Where the Aqua Sector commands the deep and the Caelum Sector the sky, Terra holds the earth itself: its forests, its mountains, its frozen marches, and the burning roads between them. Below is recorded the full disposition of each Host, its doctrine, and the standard composition of its warriors, as catalogued by the Office of the High Marshal.

II. ORDER OF BATTLE

The Seven Hosts

Each marching nation unto itself — a doctrine, a tribe of tribes, and a banner carried into every campaign of the Federation.

PERSONAL GUARD

The Golden Host

CMD. LEANDER SHAN-VALERIAN

Commanded by the Shahanshah himself, the personal guard and living exemplar of all others. Composed of elite Tahnkasha champions and mixed contingents drawn from every tribe of the Federation, it is less an army than a procession of the Empire's own image.

DOCTRINE: EXEMPLARYSTRENGTH: ~38,000
SIEGE & ATTRITION

The Iron Host

CMD. N/A

Masters of siege warfare, heavy infantry, and the patient art of earthworks. Where the Iron Host encamps, walls rise from the soil itself; where it besieges, no citadel is reckoned eternal.

DOCTRINE: ATTRITIONSTRENGTH: ~34,000
MOBILE STRIKE

The Steel Host

CMD. N/A

Renowned across Isol for mobility, armored cavalry, and precision strikes. The Steel Host does not grind down its enemies, it removes them, in a single decisive thrust, before the foe has reckoned the war begun.

DOCTRINE: PRECISIONSTRENGTH: ~30,000
NIGHT & SHADOW

The Obsidian Host

CMD. N/A

Specialists in night warfare, ambush, and the slow unraveling of an enemy's resolve. They are rarely seen and rarely heard, only felt, in the moment an army realizes it has already lost.

DOCTRINE: PSYCHOLOGICALSTRENGTH: ~26,000
SHOCK TROOPS

The Crimson Host

CMD. N/A

The shock troops of the Empire: berserkers, wolf-blooded, and fanatical zealots who measure devotion in blood spent. Where the line must break, the Crimson Host is sent first, and asks for nothing in return but the charge.

DOCTRINE: FANATICAL SHOCKSTRENGTH: ~32,000
WILDS & CANOPY

The Verdant Host

CMD. N/A

Forest and jungle specialists, skirmishers, hunters, and beast-tamers who turn the wilderness itself into a weapon. No host marches lighter, nor vanishes more completely when the canopy closes behind it.

DOCTRINE: ASYMMETRICSTRENGTH: ~24,000
NORTHERN MARCH

The White Host

CMD. N/A

Guardians of the northern marches, famed for discipline and endurance against the extreme cold of the Mandate's border. The least glorified Host, and the one the Empire can least afford to lose.

DOCTRINE: ENDURANCESTRENGTH: ~28,000
